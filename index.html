Out with the old

The old Tumbly app has a few issues.

  • The design is old and outdated
  • It can be a bit hard to read at first glance
  • It is limited to the present era
  • It does not display the final Date
  • Cannot determine a valid date

In with the New

The new Tumbly app solves them.

  • New, sleek, minimalist design
  • Easily understood on first view
  • Can be used for past, present, and the future
  • Simple, elegent display
  • Built-in date validation
